About Mr. Gulaman

Mr. Gulaman is a Filipino-inspired homemade jelly brand rooted in Cebuano culture. Since the early 90s, we have been serving freshly made gulaman sweet, colorful, and full of nostalgia straight from our family kitchen to your cup.

Every batch is made with love, using traditional recipes passed down through generations. Whether it's a classic red gulaman or a creamy buko pandan float, Mr. Gulaman is your taste of home.

👤 Meet the Creator

Daniel Diaz Lim CEO & Founder

A Cebuano student with a love for Filipino food and entreprenuership. Mr. Gulaman was created as a tribute to the classic street-side and comfort food gulaman that defined childhood summers in Philippines. The brand focuses on simple, clean elements and proudly Bisaya.
